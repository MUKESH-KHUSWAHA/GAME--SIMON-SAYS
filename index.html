How to Play

  • Press any key to start the game.
  • Watch the sequence of flashing colors.
  • Repeat the sequence by clicking the colored buttons in order.
  • Each level adds a new color to the sequence.
  • If you make a mistake, the game ends and your score is shown.
  • Try to beat your highest score!
  • Press N to view the highest score at any time.
  • Change the mode for different difficulty levels.
